SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOAL NO. 6

CLEAN WATER &
SANITATION

Clean water and sanitation are fundamental human rights and are essential for the health, dignity, and development of individuals and communities. Access to clean water means having safe, uncontaminated water available for drinking, cooking, and hygiene.

NEWS & EVENTS

Ground Water Protection and Conservation Agreement

The City Council of Baguio City approved a resolution confirming a MOA to protect groundwater resources.

Filipinos don't have access to clean and potable water

The DENR plans to procure modular desalination machines to address the lack of clean water.

Clean Water Finally

The Philippines has a clean water shortage but a new project in Davao City provides clean water and renewable energy for 1 million residents.

TRENDS & INNOVATIONS

Water Treatment Technology

Membrane filtration, ultraviolet disinfection, and nanotechnology are being used to improve water quality.

Waste Water Treatment

Wastewater treatment processes are helping to reduce pollution and recover valuable resources.

Water Harvesting

Rainwater harvesting, greywater reuse, and desalination are being explored to increase water availability.
